Digital Blockchain Assets

Analyzing top blockchain projects... and no, they're not dead.

Bitcoin

The first blockchain created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009. Genesis block read "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks."

Ethereum

Introduced in 2015 as a platform to release ICOs. Proposed by Vitalik Buterin and hardforked in 2016 into Ethereum Classic.

Ripple

Ripple is a real-time gross settlement system, currency exchange and remittance network created by Ripple Labs Inc. Seeks to replace Swift.

Cardano

3rd generation, peer reviewed and probably secure.

Maker

An Ethereum based protocol that provides an auditable stable token pegged to the USD.

Dogecoin

Meme, joke coin.
